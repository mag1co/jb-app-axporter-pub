# Privacy Policy for Article to Office Exporter

**Last Updated**: March 10, 2026

## Data Collection

**Article to Office Exporter does not collect, transmit, or store any telemetry, analytics, or personal data.**

All processing occurs locally within your YouTrack instance.

## External Services

**The plugin does not contact any external services.** All export operations (XLSX, DOCX generation) are performed entirely within the YouTrack app runtime.

## Data Processing

- The plugin reads Knowledge Base article content via YouTrack's internal API to generate export files
- Generated files are delivered directly to the user's browser as downloads
- No article content, user data, or credentials are logged or transmitted to third parties

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: apps@mag1.cc
